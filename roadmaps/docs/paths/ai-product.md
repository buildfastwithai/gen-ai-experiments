# Path: AI Product

Best for learners who want to design and ship AI features with product outcomes.

## Focus

- Use case selection
- User workflow design
- Quality, latency, and cost tradeoffs
- Safety and rollout planning

## Recommended Level Emphasis

- High focus: Levels 0, 2, 3, 4, 6
- Medium focus: Level 1 and selected topics from Level 5

## Suggested Projects

- `apps/ai-business-consultant`
- `apps/ai-ad-generator`
- `apps/stock-market-agent`
- `workshops/AI_Customer_Support_Agent_.ipynb`
- `workshops/Travel_Agent.ipynb`

## Capstone Ideas

- AI copilot for customer success
- Content generation workflow for marketing teams
- Research assistant for product managers

## Exit Criteria

- You can define success metrics, ship MVPs, and improve product quality with structured feedback.
